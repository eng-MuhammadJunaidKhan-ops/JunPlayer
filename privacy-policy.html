Privacy Policy for JunPlayer

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Introduction

JunPlayer is a media player application developed to allow users to play and manage video and audio files on their devices.

Information We Access

JunPlayer may access media files stored on your device, such as videos and audio files, in order to provide media playback and file management features.

Advertising

JunPlayer uses Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. Google may collect certain information according to its own privacy policies and advertising technologies.

Permissions

The application may request permission to access media files and other permissions necessary for the features provided by the application.

Data Collection

JunPlayer does not directly collect or store personal information on its own servers unless explicitly stated in a future update.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services, including Google Mobile Ads, which may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

JunPlayer is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information associated with the JunPlayer application.
